CBSE Board Most Repeated Numericals with Full Solution

PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. Ek p-n sandhi diode ke liye V-I abhilakshnik se, 0.7 V par dhara 20 mA hai. Yadi gatishil pratirodh 10 Ω hai to 0.75 V par dhara gyat karo.
Given: V₁ = 0.7 V, I₁ = 20 mA, r_d = 10 Ω, V₂ = 0.75 V
Step 1: Gatishil pratirodh ka sutra:
$$r_d = \frac{\Delta V}{\Delta I}$$
Step 2: Voltage me badhotri:
$$\Delta V = V_2 - V_1 = 0.75 - 0.7 = 0.05 \text{ V}$$
Step 3: Dhara me badhotri:
$$\Delta I = \frac{\Delta V}{r_d} = \frac{0.05}{10} = 0.005 \text{ A} = 5 \text{ mA}$$
Step 4: Nayi dhara:
$$I_2 = I_1 + \Delta I = 20 + 5 = 25 \text{ mA}$$
Answer: 0.75 V par dhara = 25 mA
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. Ek ardh-tarang dishtikari me niveshi a.c. ka rms maan 220 V hai. Diod aadarsh hai. Nirgat d.c. voltage gyat karo.
Given: V_rms = 220 V, Adh-tarang dishtikari
Step 1: Shikhar voltage nikalo:
$$V_m = \sqrt{2} \times V_{rms} = 1.414 \times 220 = 311 \text{ V}$$
Step 2: Adh-tarang dishtikari ke liye nirgat d.c. voltage:
$$V_{dc} = \frac{V_m}{\pi}$$
Step 3: Values rakho:
$$V_{dc} = \frac{311}{3.14} = 99 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Nirgat d.c. voltage ≈ 99 V. Ripple frequency = 50 Hz hogi.
PYQ 2024, 2022, 2019
Q3. Ek u-bhaynishth transistor pravardhak me aadhar dhara me 40 μA ka parivartan karne par sangrahak dhara me 3.2 mA ka parivartan hota hai. α aur β gyat karo.
Given: ΔI_B = 40 μA = 40 × 10⁻⁶ A, ΔI_C = 3.2 mA = 3.2 × 10⁻³ A
Step 1: Dhara laabh β ka sutra:
$$\beta = \frac{\Delta I_C}{\Delta I_B}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$\beta = \frac{3.2 \times 10^{-3}}{40 \times 10^{-6}} = \frac{3200}{40} = 80$$
Step 3: α aur β me sambandh:
$$\alpha = \frac{\beta}{1 + \beta} = \frac{80}{1 + 80} = \frac{80}{81}$$
$$\alpha = 0.988$$
Answer: β = 80, α = 0.988
PYQ 2023, 2021
Q4. Ek n-type Si me donor asuddhiyon ka saandrata 5 × 10²¹ m⁻³ hai. Kamre ke taap par naiz sahaj saandrata 1.5 × 10¹⁶ m⁻³ hai. Electron aur vivar saandrata gyat karo.
Given: N_D = 5 × 10²¹ m⁻³, n_i = 1.5 × 10¹⁶ m⁻³
Step 1: n-type me bahusankhyak wahak electron hote hain:
$$n_e \approx N_D = 5 \times 10^{21} \text{ m}^{-3}$$
Step 2: Dravyamaan kriya niyam:
$$n_e \times n_h = n_i^2$$
Step 3: Vivar saandrata nikalo:
$$n_h = \frac{n_i^2}{n_e} = \frac{(1.5 \times 10^{16})^2}{5 \times 10^{21}}$$
$$n_h = \frac{2.25 \times 10^{32}}{5 \times 10^{21}} = 4.5 \times 10^{10} \text{ m}^{-3}$$
Answer: Electron saandrata = 5 × 10²¹ m⁻³, Vivar saandrata = 4.5 × 10¹⁰ m⁻³
PYQ 2024, 2022
Q5. Ek zener diode ka bhanjan voltage 6 V hai. Yadi niveshi voltage 9 V hai aur shreni pratirodh 1 kΩ hai, to zener se pravahit dhara gyat karo. Load pratirodh 2 kΩ hai.
Given: V_z = 6 V, V_in = 9 V, R_s = 1 kΩ = 1000 Ω, R_L = 2 kΩ = 2000 Ω
Step 1: Zener breakdown me V_L = V_z = 6 V

Step 2: Load se pravahit dhara:
$$I_L = \frac{V_L}{R_L} = \frac{6}{2000} = 3 \times 10^{-3} \text{ A} = 3 \text{ mA}$$
Step 3: Shreni pratirodh se kul dhara:
$$I_S = \frac{V_{in} - V_z}{R_S} = \frac{9 - 6}{1000} = 3 \times 10^{-3} \text{ A} = 3 \text{ mA}$$
Step 4: Zener se pravahit dhara:
$$I_Z = I_S - I_L = 3 - 3 = 0 \text{ mA}$$
Answer: Zener se koi dhara nahi bahegi kyunki poori dhara load me ja rahi hai. Regulation ke liye V_in > V_z aur R_S chhota hona chahiye.
⚡ Board Tip: Diode ke liye knee voltage Si=0.7V, Ge=0.3V. Transistor: I_E = I_B + I_C, β = I_C/I_B, α = I_C/I_E. Dishtikari: Adh-tarang V_dc=V_m/π, Poorn-tarang V_dc=2V_m/π. Zener voltage regulator me V_L = V_z.
