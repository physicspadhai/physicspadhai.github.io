1. Magnetic Force on Moving Charge

Lorentz Force:

$\vec{F} = q(\vec{v} \times \vec{B})$
Magnitude: $F = qvB\sin\theta$
Direction: Right Hand Rule

2. Biot-Savart Law

Magnetic field due to current element:

$d\vec{B} = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \frac{I d\vec{l} \times \hat{r}}{r^2}$
$\mu_0 = 4\pi \times 10^{-7}$ Tm/A

Field due to straight wire:

$B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi r}$

3. Ampere's Circuital Law

$\oint \vec{B} \cdot d\vec{l} = \mu_0 I_{enc}$

Field inside Solenoid:

$B = \mu_0 n I$
n = turns per unit length

4. Force between Parallel Currents

Force per unit length:

$\frac{F}{l} = \frac{\mu_0 I_1 I_2}{2\pi d}$
Attractive if currents same direction

Previous Year Questions 2026

CBSE 2024:

Q. Derive expression for magnetic field at center of circular current loop using Biot-Savart law.

Ans: For each element, $dB = \frac{\mu_0 I dl}{4\pi R^2}$. Integrating: $B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2R}$

CBSE 2023:

Q. A proton enters magnetic field B = 0.5 T with velocity $v = 4 \times 10^6$ m/s ⊥ to B. Find radius of circular path.

Ans: $r = \frac{mv}{qB} = \frac{1.67 \times 10^{-27} \times 4 \times 10^6}{1.6 \times 10^{-19} \times 0.5} = 0.083$ m
