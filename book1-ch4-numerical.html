CBSE Board Most Repeated Numericals with Full Solution

PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. 2A ki dhara wale 10 cm trijya ke vrittaakar loop ke kendra par chumbakiya kshetra gyat karo. μ₀ = 4π × 10⁻⁷ T m/A
Given: I = 2A, r = 10 cm = 0.1 m, μ₀ = 4π × 10⁻⁷ T m/A
Step 1: Vrittaakar loop ke kendra par chumbakiya kshetra:
$$B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2r}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$B = \frac{4\pi \times 10^{-7} \times 2}{2 \times 0.1}$$
Step 3: 2 cancel ho jayega:
$$B = \frac{4\pi \times 10^{-7}}{0.1} = 40\pi \times 10^{-7}$$
Step 4: π = 3.14 rakho:
$$B = 40 \times 3.14 \times 10^{-7} = 125.6 \times 10^{-7}$$
$$B = 1.256 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$$
Answer: Chumbakiya kshetra = $1.26 \times 10^{-5}$ T, loop ke tal ke lambwat.
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2019
Q2. 5 × 10⁶ m/s ke veg se gatishil electron 0.3 T ke chumbakiya kshetra me kshetra ke lambwat pravesh karta hai. Electron ke vrittaakar path ki trijya gyat karo. mₑ = 9.1×10⁻³¹ kg, e = 1.6×10⁻¹⁹ C
Given: v = 5 × 10⁶ m/s, B = 0.3 T, θ = 90°, m = 9.1×10⁻³¹ kg, q = 1.6×10⁻¹⁹ C
Step 1: Chumbakiya kshetra me gatishil aavesh par bal:
$$F = qvB\sin\theta = qvB \text{ (kyunki sin90° = 1)}$$
Step 2: Ye bal abhikendriya bal pradan karta hai:
$$qvB = \frac{mv^2}{r}$$
Step 3: r ke liye solve karo:
$$r = \frac{mv}{qB}$$
Step 4: Values rakho:
$$r = \frac{9.1 \times 10^{-31} \times 5 \times 10^6}{1.6 \times 10^{-19} \times 0.3}$$
Step 5: Calculate karo:
$$r = \frac{45.5 \times 10^{-25}}{0.48 \times 10^{-19}} = 94.8 \times 10^{-6}$$
$$r = 9.48 \times 10^{-5} \text{ m} = 0.0948 \text{ mm}$$
Answer: Vrittaakar path ki trijya = $9.5 \times 10^{-5}$ m
PYQ 2024, 2022, 2020
Q3. Ek lambe seedhe taar me 10A ki dhara pravahit ho rahi hai. Taar se 5 cm door chumbakiya kshetra ki teevrata gyat karo.
Given: I = 10A, r = 5 cm = 0.05 m, μ₀/4π = 10⁻⁷ T m/A
Step 1: Lambe seedhe taar ke karan chumbakiya kshetra:
$$B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi r}$$
Step 2: μ₀/4π = 10⁻⁷ use karke likho:
$$B = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \times \frac{2I}{r} = 10^{-7} \times \frac{2 \times 10}{0.05}$$
Step 3: Solve karo:
$$B = 10^{-7} \times \frac{20}{0.05} = 10^{-7} \times 400$$
$$B = 4 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$$
Answer: Chumbakiya kshetra = $4 \times 10^{-5}$ T. Disha right hand thumb rule se.
PYQ 2023, 2021
Q4. 100 pheron wali 10 cm trijya ki kundli me 2A ki dhara hai. Kundli ka chumbakiya aaghurn gyat karo.
Given: N = 100, r = 10 cm = 0.1 m, I = 2A
Step 1: Kundli ka chumbakiya aaghurn:
$$M = NIA$$
Step 2: Kshetrafal A = πr²:
$$A = \pi \times (0.1)^2 = 3.14 \times 0.01 = 0.0314 \text{ m²}$$
Step 3: Values rakho:
$$M = 100 \times 2 \times 0.0314$$
$$M = 6.28 \text{ A m²}$$
Answer: Chumbakiya aaghurn = 6.28 A m²
PYQ 2024, 2022
Q5. 0.5 m lambe chalak me 5A ki dhara hai. Ise 0.2 T ke uniform chumbakiya kshetra me kshetra se 30° ke kon par rakha hai. Chalak par lagne wala bal gyat karo.
Given: l = 0.5 m, I = 5A, B = 0.2 T, θ = 30°
Step 1: Dhara wale chalak par chumbakiya bal:
$$F = BIl\sin\theta$$
Step 2: Values rakho: sin30° = 0.5
$$F = 0.2 \times 5 \times 0.5 \times 0.5$$
Step 3: Calculate karo:
$$F = 1 \times 0.25 = 0.25 \text{ N}$$
Answer: Chalak par bal = 0.25 N. Disha Fleming's left hand rule se.
