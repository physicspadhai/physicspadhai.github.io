CBSE Board Most Repeated Numericals with Full Solution

PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. 100 pheron wali kundli se baddh chumbakiya flux 0.1 s me 0.5 Wb se badhkar 1.5 Wb ho jata hai. Kundli me prerit va.va.ba. gyat karo.
Given: N = 100, φ₁ = 0.5 Wb, φ₂ = 1.5 Wb, dt = 0.1 s
Step 1: Faraday ka niyam:
$$\varepsilon = -N\frac{d\phi}{dt} = -N\frac{\phi_2 - \phi_1}{dt}$$
Step 2: Flux me parivartan:
$$d\phi = \phi_2 - \phi_1 = 1.5 - 0.5 = 1.0 \text{ Wb}$$
Step 3: Values rakho. Rinatmak chinh disha batata hai, parimaan ke liye ignore karo:
$$\varepsilon = 100 \times \frac{1.0}{0.1}$$
Step 4: Calculate karo:
$$\varepsilon = 100 \times 10 = 1000 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Prerit va.va.ba. = 1000 V
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. 0.4 m lambi chalak chhad 0.3 T ke uniform chumbakiya kshetra ke lambwat 10 m/s ke veg se gatishil hai. Chhad ke siron ke beech prerit va.va.ba. gyat karo.
Given: l = 0.4 m, B = 0.3 T, v = 10 m/s, θ = 90° kyunki kshetra ke lambwat
Step 1: Gatisheel chalak me prerit va.va.ba. ka formula:
$$\varepsilon = Blv\sin\theta$$
Step 2: θ = 90°, isliye sin90° = 1:
$$\varepsilon = Blv$$
Step 3: Values rakho:
$$\varepsilon = 0.3 \times 0.4 \times 10$$
Step 4: Calculate karo:
$$\varepsilon = 0.12 \times 10 = 1.2 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Prerit va.va.ba. = 1.2 V
PYQ 2024, 2023, 2019
Q3. Ek kundli ka swaperarkatva 5H hai. Isme dhara 0.2 s me 2A se badhkar 10A ho jati hai. Kundli me prerit va.va.ba. gyat karo.
Given: L = 5H, I₁ = 2A, I₂ = 10A, dt = 0.2 s
Step 1: Swaperarkatva ke karan prerit va.va.ba.:
$$\varepsilon = -L\frac{dI}{dt}$$
Step 2: Dhara me parivartan:
$$dI = I_2 - I_1 = 10 - 2 = 8 \text{ A}$$
Step 3: Values rakho, parimaan ke liye rinatmak chinh hatado:
$$\varepsilon = 5 \times \frac{8}{0.2}$$
Step 4: Calculate karo: 8/0.2 = 40
$$\varepsilon = 5 \times 40 = 200 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Prerit va.va.ba. = 200 V. Ye va.va.ba. dhara ke parivartan ka virodh karega.
PYQ 2023, 2022
Q4. Do samaksh kundliyon ke beech anyonaperarkatva 2H hai. Prathmik kundli me dhara 0.5 s me 5A se ghatkar 0 ho jati hai. Dwitiyak kundli me prerit va.va.ba. gyat karo.
Given: M = 2H, dI = 0 - 5 = -5A, dt = 0.5 s
Step 1: Anyonaperarkatva ke karan prerit va.va.ba.:
$$\varepsilon_2 = -M\frac{dI_1}{dt}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$\varepsilon_2 = -2 \times \frac{-5}{0.5}$$
Step 3: Solve karo: do rinatmak milke dhanyatmak:
$$\varepsilon_2 = 2 \times 10 = 20 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Dwitiyak kundli me prerit va.va.ba. = 20 V
PYQ 2024, 2021
Q5. 50 pheron wali ek kundli ka kshetrafal 0.04 m² hai. Ise 0.5 s me 0.5 T ke chumbakiya kshetra se kshetra ke lambwat se kshetra ke samantar ghumaya jata hai. Prerit ausat va.va.ba. gyat karo.
Given: N = 50, A = 0.04 m², B = 0.5 T, dt = 0.5 s
Step 1: Shuru me kundli kshetra ke lambwat hai, to θ₁ = 0°, cos0° = 1. Ant me kshetra ke samantar, to θ₂ = 90°, cos90° = 0

Step 2: Prarambhik flux:
$$\phi_1 = NBA\cos\theta_1 = 50 \times 0.5 \times 0.04 \times 1 = 1.0 \text{ Wb}$$
Step 3: Antim flux:
$$\phi_2 = NBA\cos\theta_2 = 50 \times 0.5 \times 0.04 \times 0 = 0 \text{ Wb}$$
Step 4: Prerit ausat va.va.ba.:
$$\varepsilon = N\frac{d\phi}{dt} = \frac{\phi_1 - \phi_2}{dt} = \frac{1.0 - 0}{0.5}$$
$$\varepsilon = 2.0 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Prerit ausat va.va.ba. = 2 V
⚡ Board Tip: Faraday law me rinatmak chinh Lenz niyam ke liye hai. Numerical me parimaan puchhe to rinatmak chinh hata sakte ho. Unit Wb/s = Volt yaad rakho.
