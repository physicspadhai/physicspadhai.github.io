CBSE Board Most Repeated Numericals with Full Solution

PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. 10 MHz aavritti wali vidyut-chumbakiya tarang ki tarang-dhairdhya nirvaat me gyat karo. Prakash ki chaal c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
Given: f = 10 MHz = 10 × 10⁶ Hz = 10⁷ Hz, c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
Step 1: Vidyut-chumbakiya tarang ke liye:
$$c = f\lambda$$
Step 2: λ ke liye solve karo:
$$\lambda = \frac{c}{f}$$
Step 3: Values rakho:
$$\lambda = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{10^7}$$
Step 4: Calculate karo:
$$\lambda = 3 \times 10^{8-7} = 30 \text{ m}$$
Answer: Tarang-dhairdhya = 30 m. Ye radio wave region me hai.
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. Ek samtal vidyut-chumbakiya tarang me vidyut kshetra ka aayam 6 V/m hai. Chumbakiya kshetra ka aayam gyat karo. c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
Given: E₀ = 6 V/m, c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
Step 1: EM wave me E aur B ke aayam me sambandh:
$$c = \frac{E_0}{B_0}$$
Step 2: B₀ ke liye solve karo:
$$B_0 = \frac{E_0}{c}$$
Step 3: Values rakho:
$$B_0 = \frac{6}{3 \times 10^8}$$
Step 4: Calculate karo:
$$B_0 = 2 \times 10^{-8} \text{ T}$$
Answer: Chumbakiya kshetra ka aayam = $2 \times 10^{-8}$ T
PYQ 2024, 2023, 2019
Q3. Ek sandharitra ko aaveshit kiya ja raha hai. Chaalak dhara 0.15 A hai. Visthapan dhara kitni hogi? Sandharitra ki platon ke beech chumbakiya kshetra kyu utpann hota hai?
Given: Chaalak dhara I_c = 0.15 A
Step 1: Maxwell ke anusaar Ampere-Maxwell niyam:
$$\oint \vec{B} \cdot d\vec{l} = \mu_0(I_c + I_d)$$
Step 2: Sandharitra ko aaveshit karte samay taar me chaalak dhara hai, par platon ke beech chaalak dhara nahi hai. Yaha visthapan dhara I_d kaam karti hai.

Step 3: Nirantarta ke liye:
$$I_d = I_c = 0.15 \text{ A}$$
Answer: Visthapan dhara = 0.15 A. Platon ke beech badalta vidyut kshetra visthapan dhara utpann karta hai, jo chumbakiya kshetra paida karti hai. Isse Ampere ka niyam consistent rehta hai.
PYQ 2023, 2022
Q4. Ek vidyut-chumbakiya tarang ki aavritti 5 × 10¹⁴ Hz hai. Is photon ki urja eV me gyat karo. h = 6.63 × 10⁻³⁴ J-s, 1 eV = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ J
Given: f = 5 × 10¹⁴ Hz, h = 6.63 × 10⁻³⁴ J-s
Step 1: Photon ki urja ka formula:
$$E = hf$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$E = 6.63 \times 10^{-34} \times 5 \times 10^{14}$$
Step 3: Calculate karo:
$$E = 33.15 \times 10^{-20} = 3.315 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$$
Step 4: eV me badlo:
$$E = \frac{3.315 \times 10^{-19}}{1.6 \times 10^{-19}} = 2.07 \text{ eV}$$
Answer: Photon ki urja = 2.07 eV. Ye drishya prakash region me hai.
PYQ 2024, 2021
Q5. Ek EM tarang ki teevrata 10 W/m² hai. Vidyut kshetra ka rms maan gyat karo. ε₀ = 8.85 × 10⁻¹² C²/Nm², c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
Given: I = 10 W/m², ε₀ = 8.85 × 10⁻¹² C²/Nm², c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
Step 1: EM tarang ki teevrata ka formula:
$$I = \frac{1}{2} \varepsilon_0 c E_0^2 = \varepsilon_0 c E_{rms}^2$$
Step 2: E_rms ke liye solve karo:
$$E_{rms} = \sqrt{\frac{I}{\varepsilon_0 c}}$$
Step 3: Values rakho:
$$E_{rms} = \sqrt{\frac{10}{8.85 \times 10^{-12} \times 3 \times 10^8}}$$
Step 4: Denominator: 8.85 × 3 = 26.55, 10⁻¹² × 10⁸ = 10⁻⁴
$$E_{rms} = \sqrt{\frac{10}{26.55 \times 10^{-4}}} = \sqrt{\frac{10^5}{26.55}}$$
$$E_{rms} = \sqrt{3766} = 61.4 \text{ V/m}$$
Answer: Vidyut kshetra ka rms maan = 61.4 V/m
⚡ Board Tip: c = fλ aur c = E₀/B₀ yaad rakho. Visthapan dhara = ε₀ dφ_E/dt. EM spectrum ka order yaad karo: Radio < Microwave < IR < Visible < UV < X-ray < Gamma.
