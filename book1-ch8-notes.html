1. Reflection at Spherical Mirror

Mirror Formula:

$\frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v} + \frac{1}{u}$
$f = R/2$
Magnification: $m = \frac{-v}{u} = \frac{h_i}{h_o}$

Sign Convention: All distances from pole, real +ve, virtual -ve

2. Refraction

Snell's Law:

$n_1 \sin i = n_2 \sin r$
$n_{21} = \frac{n_2}{n_1} = \frac{v_1}{v_2}$

Refraction at Spherical Surface:

$\frac{n_2}{v} - \frac{n_1}{u} = \frac{n_2 - n_1}{R}$

3. Lens

Lens Formula:

$\frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v} - \frac{1}{u}$
$m = \frac{v}{u}$

Lens Maker's Formula:

$\frac{1}{f} = (n_{21} - 1)\left(\frac{1}{R_1} - \frac{1}{R_2}\right)$

Power of Lens:

$P = \frac{1}{f(m)}$
Unit: Dioptre (D)

4. Prism

$\mu = \frac{\sin\frac{A + \delta_m}{2}}{\sin\frac{A}{2}}$
A = Angle of prism, $\delta_m$ = Min deviation

For thin prism: $\delta = (\mu - 1)A$

Previous Year Questions 2026

CBSE 2024:

Q. Derive lens maker's formula for double convex lens.

Ans: Apply refraction at 2 surfaces. For 1st: $\frac{n_2}{v_1} - \frac{n_1}{u} = \frac{n_2-n_1}{R_1}$. For 2nd: $\frac{n_1}{v} - \frac{n_2}{v_1} = \frac{n_1-n_2}{R_2}$. Adding: $\frac{1}{f} = (n_{21} - 1)(\frac{1}{R_1} - \frac{1}{R_2})$

CBSE 2023:

Q. A convex lens of focal length 20cm forms real image 2 times the object. Find object distance.

Ans: $m = -2 = v/u$ → $v = -2u$. $\frac{1}{20} = \frac{1}{-2u} - \frac{1}{u}$ → $u = -30$ cm
