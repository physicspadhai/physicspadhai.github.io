PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. Ek radioactive tatva ki ardha-aayu 6 ghante hai. 24 ghante baad uska kitna ansh avikshit rahega?
Given: T₁/₂ = 6 hours, t = 24 hours
Step 1: Kitni ardha-aayu beet gayi:
$$n = \frac{t}{T_{1/2}} = \frac{24}{6} = 4$$
Step 2: Radioactive kshay ka niyam:
$$N = N_0 \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^n$$
Step 3: n = 4 rakho:
$$\frac{N}{N_0} = \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^4 = \frac{1}{16}$$
Answer: 24 ghante baad 1/16 ansh ya 6.25% avikshit rahega.
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. ²³⁸U nabhik ki trijya gyat karo. R₀ = 1.2 × 10⁻¹⁵ m
Given: A = 238, R₀ = 1.2 fm = 1.2 × 10⁻¹⁵ m
Step 1: Nabhik ki trijya ka sutra:
$$R = R_0 A^{1/3}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$R = 1.2 \times 10^{-15} \times (238)^{1/3}$$
Step 3: (238)¹/³ ≈ 6.2:
$$R = 1.2 \times 10^{-15} \times 6.2$$
$$R = 7.44 \times 10^{-15} \text{ m} = 7.44 \text{ fm}$$
Answer: ²³⁸U nabhik ki trijya ≈ 7.44 fm
PYQ 2024, 2023, 2019
Q3. ⁴He nabhik ki bandhan urja gyat karo. m_p = 1.00727 u, m_n = 1.00866 u, m_He = 4.00260 u, 1u = 931.5 MeV/c²
Given: Z = 2, N = 2, A = 4
Step 1: Dravyamaan-kshati nikal:
$$\Delta m = [Zm_p + Nm_n] - M_{nabhik}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$\Delta m = [2(1.00727) + 2(1.00866)] - 4.00260$$
$$\Delta m = [2.01454 + 2.01732] - 4.00260$$
$$\Delta m = 4.03186 - 4.00260 = 0.02926 \text{ u}$$
Step 3: Bandhan urja:
$$B.E. = \Delta m \times 931.5 \text{ MeV}$$
$$B.E. = 0.02926 \times 931.5 = 27.25 \text{ MeV}$$
Step 4: Prati nucleon bandhan urja:
$$\frac{B.E.}{A} = \frac{27.25}{4} = 6.81 \text{ MeV/nucleon}$$
Answer: Bandhan urja = 27.25 MeV, Prati nucleon = 6.81 MeV
PYQ 2023, 2022
Q4. Ek radioactive padarth ki sakriyata 5 din me 8000 counts/sec se ghatkar 1000 counts/sec ho jaati hai. Iski ardha-aayu gyat karo.
Given: R₀ = 8000, R = 1000, t = 5 din
Step 1: Sakriyata ka niyam:
$$R = R_0 \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{t/T_{1/2}}$$
Step 2: Ratio nikalo:
$$\frac{R}{R_0} = \frac{1000}{8000} = \frac{1}{8} = \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^3$$
Step 3: Compare karo:
$$\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{t/T_{1/2}} = \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^3$$
$$\frac{t}{T_{1/2}} = 3$$
Step 4: T₁/₂ nikalo:
$$T_{1/2} = \frac{t}{3} = \frac{5}{3} = 1.67 \text{ din}$$
Answer: Ardha-aayu = 1.67 din ≈ 40 ghante
PYQ 2024, 2021
Q5. Abhikriya ²H + ³H → ⁴He + n + Q me vimukt urja Q gyat karo. m(²H) = 2.0141 u, m(³H) = 3.0160 u, m(⁴He) = 4.0026 u, m_n = 1.0087 u, 1u = 931.5 MeV/c²
Given: Deuterium + Tritium → Helium + neutron
Step 1: Abhikriya se pehle kul dravyamaan:
$$m_i = 2.0141 + 3.0160 = 5.0301 \text{ u}$$
Step 2: Abhikriya ke baad kul dravyamaan:
$$m_f = 4.0026 + 1.0087 = 5.0113 \text{ u}$$
Step 3: Dravyamaan-kshati:
$$\Delta m = m_i - m_f = 5.0301 - 5.0113 = 0.0188 \text{ u}$$
Step 4: Vimukt urja Q:
$$Q = \Delta m \times 931.5 = 0.0188 \times 931.5$$
$$Q = 17.51 \text{ MeV}$$
Answer: Is sangalan abhikriya me 17.51 MeV urja vimukt hogi. Ye hydrogen bomb ki abhikriya hai.
⚡ Board Tip: N = N₀(1/2)^n yaad rakho jaha n = t/T₁/₂. Bandhan urja me Δm = Zm_p + Nm_n - M. 1u = 931.5 MeV. Nabhik ki trijya R = R₀A¹/³. α-kshay me A-4, Z-2. β⁻-kshay me Z+1.
