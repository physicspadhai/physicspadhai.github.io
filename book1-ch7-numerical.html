Chapter 7: Alternating Current - Important Numericals 🧮

CBSE Board Most Repeated Numericals with Full Solution

PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. 100Ω ke pratirodh ko 220V, 50Hz AC supply se joda gaya hai. Paripath me rms dhara aur shirsh dhara gyat karo.
Given: R = 100Ω, V_rms = 220V, f = 50Hz
Step 1: Pure pratirodhiya paripath ke liye Ohm's law:
$$I_{rms} = \frac{V_{rms}}{R}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$I_{rms} = \frac{220}{100} = 2.2 \text{ A}$$
Step 3: Shirsh dhara aur rms dhara me sambandh:
$$I_0 = \sqrt{2} \times I_{rms}$$
Step 4: √2 = 1.414 use karo:
$$I_0 = 1.414 \times 2.2 = 3.11 \text{ A}$$
Answer: RMS dhara = 2.2 A, Shirsh dhara = 3.11 A
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. 0.1H ki aadarsh kundli 200V, 50Hz AC supply se jodi gayi hai. Prerit pratighat aur rms dhara gyat karo.
Given: L = 0.1H, V_rms = 200V, f = 50Hz
Step 1: Prerit pratighat ka formula:
$$X_L = \omega L = 2\pi f L$$
Step 2: Values rakho: π = 3.14
$$X_L = 2 \times 3.14 \times 50 \times 0.1$$
Step 3: Calculate karo:
$$X_L = 6.28 \times 5 = 31.4 \Omega$$
Step 4: RMS dhara:
$$I_{rms} = \frac{V_{rms}}{X_L} = \frac{200}{31.4} = 6.37 \text{ A}$$
Answer: Prerit pratighat = 31.4 Ω, RMS dhara = 6.37 A. Note: Dhara voltage se 90° pichhe rahegi.
PYQ 2024, 2023, 2019
Q3. 15μF ke sandharitra ko 220V, 50Hz supply se joda gaya hai. Dharitiya pratighat aur rms dhara gyat karo.
Given: C = 15μF = 15 × 10⁻⁶ F, V_rms = 220V, f = 50Hz
Step 1: Dharitiya pratighat:
$$X_C = \frac{1}{\omega C} = \frac{1}{2\pi f C}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$X_C = \frac{1}{2 \times 3.14 \times 50 \times 15 \times 10^{-6}}$$
Step 3: Denominator solve karo:
$$X_C = \frac{1}{314 \times 15 \times 10^{-6}} = \frac{1}{4710 \times 10^{-6}}$$
$$X_C = \frac{10^6}{4710} = 212.3 \Omega$$
Step 4: RMS dhara:
$$I_{rms} = \frac{V_{rms}}{X_C} = \frac{220}{212.3} = 1.04 \text{ A}$$
Answer: Dharitiya pratighat = 212.3 Ω, RMS dhara = 1.04 A. Dhara voltage se 90° aage rahegi.
PYQ 2023, 2022
Q4. LCR shreni paripath me R = 100Ω, L = 0.2H, C = 20μF aur supply 220V, 50Hz hai. Paripath ki pratibaadha aur dhara gyat karo.
Given: R = 100Ω, L = 0.2H, C = 20μF = 20×10⁻⁶F, V = 220V, f = 50Hz
Step 1: Prerit pratighat:
$$X_L = 2\pi f L = 2 \times 3.14 \times 50 \times 0.2 = 62.8 \Omega$$
Step 2: Dharitiya pratighat:
$$X_C = \frac{1}{2\pi f C} = \frac{1}{2 \times 3.14 \times 50 \times 20 \times 10^{-6}}$$
$$X_C = \frac{1}{6280 \times 10^{-6}} = 159.2 \Omega$$
Step 3: Pratibaadha:
$$Z = \sqrt{R^2 + (X_L - X_C)^2}$$
$$Z = \sqrt{100^2 + (62.8 - 159.2)^2} = \sqrt{10000 + (-96.4)^2}$$
$$Z = \sqrt{10000 + 9293} = \sqrt{19293} = 138.9 \Omega$$
Step 4: Dhara:
$$I_{rms} = \frac{V_{rms}}{Z} = \frac{220}{138.9} = 1.58 \text{ A}$$
Answer: Pratibaadha = 138.9 Ω, Dhara = 1.58 A
PYQ 2024, 2022
Q5. Ek transformer ki prathmik kundli me 200 phere aur dwitiyak me 1000 phere hain. Yadi prathmik par 220V AC lagaye to dwitiyak me output voltage kitna hoga? Yadi output 2A le to input dhara kitni hogi? Transformer aadarsh maano.
Given: Nₚ = 200, Nₛ = 1000, Vₚ = 220V, Iₛ = 2A
Step 1: Transformer ka sutra:
$$\frac{V_s}{V_p} = \frac{N_s}{N_p}$$
Step 2: Output voltage:
$$V_s = V_p \times \frac{N_s}{N_p} = 220 \times \frac{1000}{200}$$
$$V_s = 220 \times 5 = 1100 \text{ V}$$
Step 3: Aadarsh transformer ke liye input power = output power:
$$V_p I_p = V_s I_s$$
Step 4: Input dhara:
$$I_p = \frac{V_s I_s}{V_p} = \frac{1100 \times 2}{220} = 10 \text{ A}$$
Answer: Output voltage = 1100 V, Input dhara = 10 A. Ye uchchayak transformer hai.
⚡ Board Tip: AC me hamesha rms values use hoti hain jab tak shirsh value na maangi jaye. LCR me phase difference ka dhyan rakho: X_L > X_C to dhara pichhe, X_C > X_L to dhara aage.
