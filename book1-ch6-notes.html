Chapter 6: Electromagnetic Induction ⚡🧲

1. Magnetic Flux

Total number of magnetic field lines passing normally through a surface.

$$\phi_B = \vec{B} \cdot \vec{A} = BA\cos\theta$$ SI Unit: Weber (Wb) = T-m² = V-s
🔥 Board Point: Flux maximum when $\theta = 0°$, flux zero when $\theta = 90°$. Flux is scalar quantity.

2. Faraday's Law of EMI

Whenever magnetic flux linked with a circuit changes, an EMF is induced in it.

$$e = -\frac{d\phi_B}{dt}$$ For N turns: $$e = -N\frac{d\phi_B}{dt}$$

Lenz's Law:

The polarity of induced EMF is such that it opposes the change in magnetic flux that produces it. Minus sign in Faraday's law represents Lenz's law.

🔥 Board Point: Lenz's law follows Law of Conservation of Energy. Agar oppose nahi karega to energy create ho jayegi from nothing.

3. Methods to Induce EMF

EMF induce karne ke 3 tarike: $\phi_B = BA\cos\theta$

  • 1. B change karke: Magnet ko coil ke paas le jaao
  • 2. A change karke: Coil ka area change karo
  • 3. θ change karke: Coil ko rotate karo

4. Motional EMF

Conductor rod of length l moving with velocity v perpendicular to uniform B field:

$$e = Blv$$ If velocity at angle θ: $$e = Blv\sin\theta$$

Fleming's Right Hand Rule:

Thumb → Motion, Forefinger → Field, Middle finger → Induced Current/EMF

PYQ 2023:

Q. A rod 0.5m long moves with 2 m/s in field B = 0.3 T. Find induced EMF.
Ans: $e = Blv = 0.3 \times 0.5 \times 2 = 0.3$ V

5. Energy Consideration

Mechanical power = Electrical power

$$P_{mech} = Fv = IlB \times v = I(Blv) = Ie = P_{elec}$$

6. Eddy Currents

Bulk pieces of conductor me induce hone wale circulating currents jab magnetic flux change hota hai.

Disadvantages:

  • Heat loss $I^2R$ - Transformer core garam ho jata hai
  • Damping effect

Reducing Eddy Currents:

Laminated core use karo - thin sheets insulated from each other.

Applications:

  • Induction furnace
  • Electromagnetic damping
  • Electric brakes in trains
  • Speedometer

7. Self Induction

Coil me current change hone se usi coil me EMF induce hona.

$$\phi_B \propto I \Rightarrow \phi_B = LI$$ $$e = -L\frac{dI}{dt}$$ L = Self Inductance. Unit: Henry (H)

Self Inductance of Solenoid:

$$L = \frac{\mu_0 N^2 A}{l} = \mu_0 n^2 Al$$ n = turns per unit length

Energy Stored in Inductor:

$$U = \frac{1}{2}LI^2$$

PYQ 2024:

Q. Define self inductance. Write SI unit. Derive expression for solenoid.
Ans: $L = \phi/I$. Unit Henry. For solenoid $B = \mu_0nI$, $\phi = NBA$, $L = \mu_0n^2Al$

8. Mutual Induction

Ek coil me current change hone se doosri coil me EMF induce hona.

$$\phi_2 \propto I_1 \Rightarrow \phi_2 = MI_1$$ $$e_2 = -M\frac{dI_1}{dt}$$ M = Mutual Inductance. Unit: Henry (H)

Mutual Inductance of Two Coaxial Solenoids:

$$M = \frac{\mu_0 N_1 N_2 A}{l} = \mu_0 n_1 n_2 Al$$

Coefficient of Coupling (K):

$$K = \frac{M}{\sqrt{L_1 L_2}}$$ For perfect coupling K = 1, for no coupling K = 0

9. AC Generator / Dynamo

Mechanical energy ko electrical energy me convert karta hai. Principle: EMI

$$e = NBA\omega\sin\omega t$$ $$e_{max} = NBA\omega$$ $$e_{rms} = \frac{e_{max}}{\sqrt{2}}$$
