1. AC Voltage & Current

$V = V_0 \sin\omega t$
$I = I_0 \sin\omega t$
$\omega = 2\pi f$

Mean & RMS Values:

$I_{rms} = \frac{I_0}{\sqrt{2}} = 0.707 I_0$
$V_{rms} = \frac{V_0}{\sqrt{2}} = 0.707 V_0$

2. AC Circuit Elements

ElementReactancePhase b/w V & I
Resistor R$R$V & I in phase
Inductor L$X_L = \omega L$V leads I by 90°
Capacitor C$X_C = \frac{1}{\omega C}$I leads V by 90°

3. LCR Series Circuit

Impedance:

$Z = \sqrt{R^2 + (X_L - X_C)^2}$
$I_0 = \frac{V_0}{Z}$

Phase Angle:

$\tan\phi = \frac{X_L - X_C}{R}$

Resonance:

$X_L = X_C$ → $Z_{min} = R$, $I_{max}$

$f_0 = \frac{1}{2\pi\sqrt{LC}}$ = Resonant Frequency

4. Power in AC Circuit

$P_{avg} = V_{rms} I_{rms} \cos\phi$
$\cos\phi$ = Power Factor

Wattless Current: For pure L or C, $\phi = 90°$, $P = 0$

5. Transformer

Principle: Mutual Induction

$\frac{V_s}{V_p} = \frac{N_s}{N_p} = \frac{I_p}{I_s}$ for ideal

Step-up: $N_s > N_p$, $V_s > V_p$
Step-down: $N_s < N_p$, $V_s < V_p$

Previous Year Questions 2026

CBSE 2024:

Q. An LCR series circuit with R=100Ω, L=0.5H, C=10μF connected to 220V, 50Hz. Find impedance & phase angle.

Ans: $X_L = 157Ω$, $X_C = 318Ω$, $Z = \sqrt{100^2 + (157-318)^2} = 189Ω$. $\phi = \tan^{-1}(-1.61) = -58°$

CBSE 2023:

Q. Define wattless current. In which circuit it occurs?

Ans: Current in AC circuit when avg power = 0. Occurs in pure inductive or pure capacitive circuit where $\phi = 90°$
