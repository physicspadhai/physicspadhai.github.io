CBSE Board Most Repeated Numericals with Full Solution

PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. 10Ω aur 20Ω ke do pratirodh samanantar kram me jode gaye hain. Sanyojan ka tulya pratirodh gyat karo. Yadi 6V ki battery jodi jaye to kul dhara kitni hogi?
Given: R₁ = 10Ω, R₂ = 20Ω, V = 6V, Samanantar kram
Step 1: Samanantar kram me tulya pratirodh:
$$\frac{1}{R_p} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$\frac{1}{R_p} = \frac{1}{10} + \frac{1}{20} = \frac{2+1}{20} = \frac{3}{20}$$
Step 3: Isliye Rₚ = $\frac{20}{3}$ = 6.67 Ω

Step 4: Ohm's law se kul dhara:
$$I = \frac{V}{R_p} = \frac{6}{20/3} = \frac{6 \times 3}{20} = \frac{18}{20} = 0.9 \text{ A}$$
Answer: Tulya pratirodh = 6.67 Ω, Kul dhara = 0.9 A
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. Ek taar ka pratirodh 4Ω hai. Isse khinch kar iski lambai dugni kar di jaye to naya pratirodh kitna hoga?
Given: R₁ = 4Ω, l₂ = 2l₁, Volume same rahega
Step 1: Pratirodh ka formula:
$$R = \rho \frac{l}{A}$$
Step 2: Volume same hai isliye A₁l₁ = A₂l₂
$$A_2 = \frac{A_1 l_1}{l_2} = \frac{A_1 l_1}{2l_1} = \frac{A_1}{2}$$
Step 3: Naya pratirodh:
$$R_2 = \rho \frac{l_2}{A_2} = \rho \frac{2l_1}{A_1/2} = \rho \frac{4l_1}{A_1} = 4R_1$$
Step 4: Values rakho:
$$R_2 = 4 \times 4 = 16 \Omega$$
Answer: Naya pratirodh = 16 Ω. Note: Lambai n guna ho to pratirodh n² guna ho jata hai.
PYQ 2024, 2023, 2019
Q3. Ek cell ka emf 2V hai aur aantrik pratirodh 0.1Ω hai. Ise 3.9Ω ke bahya pratirodh se joda gaya hai. Terminal voltage gyat karo.
Given: E = 2V, r = 0.1Ω, R = 3.9Ω
Step 1: Circuit me kul dhara:
$$I = \frac{E}{R + r}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$I = \frac{2}{3.9 + 0.1} = \frac{2}{4} = 0.5 \text{ A}$$
Step 3: Terminal voltage V = IR ya V = E - Ir:
$$V = E - Ir = 2 - 0.5 \times 0.1$$
Step 4: Calculate karo:
$$V = 2 - 0.05 = 1.95 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Terminal voltage = 1.95 V
PYQ 2023, 2022
Q4. Wheatstone bridge me P=2Ω, Q=3Ω, R=4Ω hai. Bridge santulit hai to S ka maan kitna hoga?
Given: P = 2Ω, Q = 3Ω, R = 4Ω, Bridge santulit
Step 1: Wheatstone bridge ke santulan ki shart:
$$\frac{P}{Q} = \frac{R}{S}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$\frac{2}{3} = \frac{4}{S}$$
Step 3: Cross multiply karo:
$$2S = 12$$
$$S = 6 \Omega$$
Answer: S = 6 Ω
PYQ 2024, 2021
Q5. 100W, 220V ke bulb ka pratirodh kitna hoga? Yadi ise 110V par chalaye to kitni power consume karega?
Given: P = 100W, V = 220V, V' = 110V
Step 1: Power ka formula: $P = \frac{V^2}{R}$ isliye:
$$R = \frac{V^2}{P} = \frac{220^2}{100}$$
Step 2: Calculate karo: 220² = 48400
$$R = \frac{48400}{100} = 484 \Omega$$
Step 3: 110V par power:
$$P' = \frac{V'^2}{R} = \frac{110^2}{484}$$
Step 4: 110² = 12100:
$$P' = \frac{12100}{484} = 25 \text{ W}$$
Answer: Pratirodh = 484 Ω, 110V par power = 25 W. Note: Voltage aadha to power 1/4 ho gayi.
