CBSE Board Most Repeated Numericals with Full Solution

PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. Ek chhoti chumbak jiska chumbakiya aaghurn 0.48 J/T hai, ke aksh par 10 cm door chumbakiya kshetra gyat karo. μ₀/4π = 10⁻⁷ T m/A
Given: M = 0.48 J/T, r = 10 cm = 0.1 m, μ₀/4π = 10⁻⁷ T m/A
Step 1: Chumbakiya dwidhruv ke aksh par kshetra:
$$B_{axial} = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \frac{2M}{r^3}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$B_{axial} = 10^{-7} \times \frac{2 \times 0.48}{(0.1)^3}$$
Step 3: (0.1)³ = 0.001 solve karo:
$$B_{axial} = 10^{-7} \times \frac{0.96}{0.001}$$
Step 4: 0.96/0.001 = 960:
$$B_{axial} = 10^{-7} \times 960 = 9.6 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$$
Answer: Chumbakiya kshetra = $9.6 \times 10^{-5}$ T, chumbak ki disha me.
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. Upar wali chumbak ke nirakshiya rekha par 10 cm door chumbakiya kshetra kitna hoga?
Given: M = 0.48 J/T, r = 0.1 m
Step 1: Nirakshiya rekha par kshetra:
$$B_{equatorial} = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \frac{M}{r^3}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$B_{equatorial} = 10^{-7} \times \frac{0.48}{(0.1)^3}$$
Step 3: Solve karo:
$$B_{equatorial} = 10^{-7} \times \frac{0.48}{0.001} = 10^{-7} \times 480$$
$$B_{equatorial} = 4.8 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$$
Answer: Kshetra = $4.8 \times 10^{-5}$ T. Note: B_axial = 2 × B_equatorial
PYQ 2024, 2022, 2019
Q3. Ek dand chumbak ko 0.2 T ke uniform chumbakiya kshetra me kshetra se 30° ke kon par rakha hai. Chumbak par 0.2 N-m ka bal aaghurn lagta hai. Chumbak ka chumbakiya aaghurn gyat karo.
Given: B = 0.2 T, θ = 30°, τ = 0.2 N-m
Step 1: Chumbakiya kshetra me chumbak par bal aaghurn:
$$\tau = MB\sin\theta$$
Step 2: M ke liye solve karo:
$$M = \frac{\tau}{B\sin\theta}$$
Step 3: Values rakho: sin30° = 0.5
$$M = \frac{0.2}{0.2 \times 0.5} = \frac{0.2}{0.1}$$
$$M = 2 \text{ J/T}$$
Answer: Chumbakiya aaghurn = 2 J/T ya 2 A m²
PYQ 2023, 2021
Q4. Ek paramagnetic padarth ke liye 300K par chumbakiya pravritti 2.4 × 10⁻⁵ hai. 150K par pravritti kitni hogi?
Given: χ₁ = 2.4 × 10⁻⁵, T₁ = 300K, T₂ = 150K
Step 1: Curie ka niyam paramagnetic padarth ke liye:
$$\chi \propto \frac{1}{T} \Rightarrow \chi T = \text{constant}$$
Step 2: Isliye χ₁T₁ = χ₂T₂:
$$\chi_2 = \frac{\chi_1 T_1}{T_2}$$
Step 3: Values rakho:
$$\chi_2 = \frac{2.4 \times 10^{-5} \times 300}{150}$$
Step 4: 300/150 = 2:
$$\chi_2 = 2.4 \times 10^{-5} \times 2 = 4.8 \times 10^{-5}$$
Answer: 150K par pravritti = $4.8 \times 10^{-5}$. Note: Temperature aadha to pravritti dugni.
PYQ 2024, 2022
Q5. Ek parinaalika ke andar lohe ki chhad rakhne par chumbakiya kshetra 1000 guna badh jata hai. Lohe ki saapeksh chumbakshilta aur chumbakiya pravritti gyat karo.
Given: B = 1000 B₀
Step 1: Saapeksh chumbakshilta ki paribhasha:
$$\mu_r = \frac{B}{B_0} = 1000$$
Step 2: Chumbakiya pravritti aur saapeksh chumbakshilta me sambandh:
$$\mu_r = 1 + \chi_m$$
Step 3: χₘ ke liye solve karo:
$$\chi_m = \mu_r - 1 = 1000 - 1 = 999$$
Answer: Saapeksh chumbakshilta μᵣ = 1000, Chumbakiya pravritti χₘ = 999. Ye lauh-chumbakiya padarth hai.
⚡ Board Tip: B_axial = 2 × B_equatorial yaad rakho. Curie law me χ ∝ 1/T. Laauh-chumbakiya ke liye χₘ bahut bada hota hai.
