CBSE Board Most Repeated Numericals with Full Solution

PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. Hydrogen parmanu ki pratham kaksha ki trijya 0.53 Å hai. Teesri kaksha ki trijya gyat karo.
Given: r₁ = 0.53 Å, n = 3
Step 1: Bohr model ke anusaar n vi kaksha ki trijya:
$$r_n = n^2 r_1$$
Step 2: n = 3 ke liye values rakho:
$$r_3 = 3^2 \times 0.53$$
Step 3: Calculate karo:
$$r_3 = 9 \times 0.53 = 4.77 \text{ Å}$$
Answer: Teesri kaksha ki trijya = 4.77 Å = $4.77 \times 10^{-10}$ m
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. Hydrogen parmanu ki bhu-avastha me electron ki urja -13.6 eV hai. Pratham uttejit avastha me electron ki urja aur uski gatij urja gyat karo.
Given: E₁ = -13.6 eV. Pratham uttejit avastha n = 2
Step 1: n vi kaksha me electron ki urja:
$$E_n = \frac{E_1}{n^2}$$
Step 2: n = 2 ke liye:
$$E_2 = \frac{-13.6}{2^2} = \frac{-13.6}{4} = -3.4 \text{ eV}$$
Step 3: Bohr model me gatij urja:
$$K.E. = -E_n$$
$$K.E. = -(-3.4) = 3.4 \text{ eV}$$
Answer: Pratham uttejit avastha me urja = -3.4 eV, Gatij urja = 3.4 eV
PYQ 2024, 2022, 2019
Q3. Hydrogen parmanu me electron n = 3 se n = 2 me sankraman karta hai. Utsarjit photon ki tarang-dhairdhya gyat karo. R = 1.097 × 10⁷ m⁻¹
Given: n_i = 3, n_f = 2, R = 1.097 × 10⁷ m⁻¹
Step 1: Rydberg sutra:
$$\frac{1}{\lambda} = R\left(\frac{1}{n_f^2} - \frac{1}{n_i^2}\right)$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$\frac{1}{\lambda} = 1.097 \times 10^7\left(\frac{1}{2^2} - \frac{1}{3^2}\right)$$
Step 3: Solve karo:
$$\frac{1}{\lambda} = 1.097 \times 10^7\left(\frac{1}{4} - \frac{1}{9}\right)$$
$$\frac{1}{\lambda} = 1.097 \times 10^7\left(\frac{9-4}{36}\right) = 1.097 \times 10^7 \times \frac{5}{36}$$
$$\frac{1}{\lambda} = 1.523 \times 10^6$$
Step 4: λ nikalo:
$$\lambda = \frac{1}{1.523 \times 10^6} = 6.56 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m} = 656 \text{ nm}$$
Answer: Tarang-dhairdhya = 656 nm. Ye Balmer shreni ki H-alpha line hai, laal rang.
PYQ 2023, 2021
Q4. Hydrogen parmanu ki bhu-avastha se electron ko mukt karane ke liye kitni urja chahiye? Ionization potential kitna hoga?
Given: E₁ = -13.6 eV
Step 1: Ionization urja = Electron ko n = 1 se n = ∞ tak le jane ki urja:
$$\Delta E = E_{\infty} - E_1$$
Step 2: n = ∞ par E_∞ = 0:
$$\Delta E = 0 - (-13.6) = 13.6 \text{ eV}$$
Step 3: Ionization potential:
$$V_{ion} = \frac{\Delta E}{e} = \frac{13.6 \text{ eV}}{e} = 13.6 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Ionization urja = 13.6 eV, Ionization potential = 13.6 V
PYQ 2024, 2022
Q5. He⁺ aayan ki pratham kaksha ki trijya hydrogen ki pratham kaksha se kitni guna hai? He⁺ ki bhu-avastha urja kitni hogi?
Given: He⁺ ke liye Z = 2, Hydrogen ke liye Z = 1
Step 1: Bohr trijya ka sutra:
$$r_n = \frac{n^2}{Z} \times 0.53 \text{ Å}$$
Step 2: n = 1 ke liye He⁺ ki trijya:
$$r_{He^+} = \frac{1^2}{2} \times 0.53 = 0.265 \text{ Å}$$
Step 3: Anupaat:
$$\frac{r_{He^+}}{r_H} = \frac{0.265}{0.53} = \frac{1}{2}$$
Step 4: Urja ka sutra:
$$E_n = -13.6 \times \frac{Z^2}{n^2} \text{ eV}$$
$$E_1(He^+) = -13.6 \times \frac{2^2}{1^2} = -13.6 \times 4 = -54.4 \text{ eV}$$
Answer: He⁺ ki pratham kaksha ki trijya hydrogen se aadhi hai. Bhu-avastha urja = -54.4 eV
⚡ Board Tip: r_n = n²×0.53 Å, E_n = -13.6/n² eV, v_n = 2.18×10⁶/n m/s yaad rakho. Hydrogen-like ion ke liye Z² se multiply aur Z se divide karo. Lyman: n→1, Balmer: n→2, Paschen: n→3.
