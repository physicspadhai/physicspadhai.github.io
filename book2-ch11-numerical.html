PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. 2.14 eV kaarya-phalan wali dhatu par 4.0 eV urja ke photon aapatit hote hain. Utsarjit photoelectron ki adhiktam gatij urja aur nirodhi vibhav gyat karo.
Given: φ₀ = 2.14 eV, hν = 4.0 eV, e = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ C
Step 1: Einstein ka prakash-vidyut samikaran:
$$K_{max} = h\nu - \phi_0$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$K_{max} = 4.0 - 2.14 = 1.86 \text{ eV}$$
Step 3: Nirodhi vibhav V₀ ke liye:
$$eV_0 = K_{max}$$
$$V_0 = \frac{K_{max}}{e} = \frac{1.86 \text{ eV}}{e} = 1.86 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Adhiktam gatij urja = 1.86 eV, Nirodhi vibhav = 1.86 V
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. 400 nm tarang-dhairdhya wale photon ki urja eV me aur sanveg gyat karo. h = 6.63 × 10⁻³⁴ J-s, c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
Given: λ = 400 nm = 400 × 10⁻⁹ m = 4 × 10⁻⁷ m
Step 1: Photon ki urja:
$$E = \frac{hc}{\lambda}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$E = \frac{6.63 \times 10^{-34} \times 3 \times 10^8}{4 \times 10^{-7}}$$
Step 3: Calculate karo:
$$E = \frac{19.89 \times 10^{-26}}{4 \times 10^{-7}} = 4.97 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$$
Step 4: eV me badlo: 1 eV = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ J
$$E = \frac{4.97 \times 10^{-19}}{1.6 \times 10^{-19}} = 3.11 \text{ eV}$$
Step 5: Photon ka sanveg:
$$p = \frac{h}{\lambda} = \frac{6.63 \times 10^{-34}}{4 \times 10^{-7}} = 1.66 \times 10^{-27} \text{ kg m/s}$$
Answer: Urja = 3.11 eV, Sanveg = $1.66 \times 10^{-27}$ kg m/s
PYQ 2024, 2023, 2019
Q3. Ek electron 100 V vibhavantar se tvarit kiya jata hai. Is electron se sambandh de-Broglie tarang-dhairdhya gyat karo. mₑ = 9.1 × 10⁻³¹ kg, h = 6.63 × 10⁻³⁴ J-s
Given: V = 100 V, m = 9.1 × 10⁻³¹ kg
Step 1: Tvarit electron ki gatij urja:
$$K = eV = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$$
$$v = \sqrt{\frac{2eV}{m}}$$
Step 2: de-Broglie tarang-dhairdhya:
$$\lambda = \frac{h}{p} = \frac{h}{mv} = \frac{h}{\sqrt{2meV}}$$
Step 3: Electron ke liye shortcut formula: λ = 12.27/√V Å
$$\lambda = \frac{12.27}{\sqrt{100}} = \frac{12.27}{10} = 1.227 \text{ Å}$$
Step 4: Metre me:
$$\lambda = 1.227 \times 10^{-10} \text{ m} = 0.1227 \text{ nm}$$
Answer: de-Broglie tarang-dhairdhya = 1.227 Å = 0.1227 nm
PYQ 2023, 2022
Q4. Ek dhatu ka kaarya-phalan 2.3 eV hai. Yadi is par 300 nm ka prakash dala jaye to kya photoelectron utsarjit honge? Yadi haan to unki adhiktam gatij urja kitni hogi? h = 6.63 × 10⁻³⁴ J-s
Given: φ₀ = 2.3 eV, λ = 300 nm = 3 × 10⁻⁷ m
Step 1: Aapatit photon ki urja:
$$E = \frac{hc}{\lambda} = \frac{6.63 \times 10^{-34} \times 3 \times 10^8}{3 \times 10^{-7}}$$
$$E = 6.63 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J} = \frac{6.63 \times 10^{-19}}{1.6 \times 10^{-19}} = 4.14 \text{ eV}$$
Step 2: Dehali urja = Kaarya-phalan = 2.3 eV. Kyunki 4.14 eV > 2.3 eV, photoelectron utsarjit honge.

Step 3: Adhiktam gatij urja:
$$K_{max} = h\nu - \phi_0 = 4.14 - 2.3 = 1.84 \text{ eV}$$
Answer: Haan, photoelectron utsarjit honge. K_max = 1.84 eV
PYQ 2024, 2021
Q5. Ek proton aur ek alpha-kann ki gatij urja barabar hai. Unki de-Broglie tarang-dhairdhyaon ka anupaat gyat karo.
Given: K_proton = K_alpha, m_alpha = 4m_p, q_alpha = 2e
Step 1: Gatij urja aur sanveg me sambandh:
$$K = \frac{p^2}{2m} \Rightarrow p = \sqrt{2mK}$$
Step 2: de-Broglie tarang-dhairdhya:
$$\lambda = \frac{h}{p} = \frac{h}{\sqrt{2mK}}$$
Step 3: K samaan hai, to λ ∝ 1/√m:
$$\frac{\lambda_p}{\lambda_\alpha} = \frac{\sqrt{2m_\alpha K}}{\sqrt{2m_p K}} = \sqrt{\frac{m_\alpha}{m_p}}$$
Step 4: m_alpha = 4m_p rakho:
$$\frac{\lambda_p}{\lambda_\alpha} = \sqrt{\frac{4m_p}{m_p}} = \sqrt{4} = \frac{2}{1}$$
Answer: λ_proton : λ_alpha = 2 : 1. Proton ki tarang-dhairdhya dugni hogi.
⚡ Board Tip: 1 eV = 1.6×10⁻¹⁹ J yaad rakho. Electron ke liye λ = 12.27/√V Å. Photoelectric ke liye K_max = hν - φ₀. de-Broglie: λ = h/p = h/√(2mK).
