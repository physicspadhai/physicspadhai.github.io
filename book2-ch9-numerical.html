CBSE Board Most Repeated Numericals with Full Solution

PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. 20 cm focus doori wale avatal darpan ke samne 30 cm door 5 cm unchi vastu rakhi hai. Pratibimb ki sthiti, prakriti aur size gyat karo.
Given: f = -20 cm, u = -30 cm, h₀ = 5 cm. Sign convention: Concave mirror ke liye f negative
Step 1: Darpan sutra:
$$\frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v} + \frac{1}{u}$$
Step 2: 1/v ke liye solve karo:
$$\frac{1}{v} = \frac{1}{f} - \frac{1}{u} = \frac{1}{-20} - \frac{1}{-30}$$
Step 3: LCM 60 leke solve karo:
$$\frac{1}{v} = \frac{-3 + 2}{60} = \frac{-1}{60}$$
$$v = -60 \text{ cm}$$
Step 4: Aavardhan:
$$m = -\frac{v}{u} = -\frac{-60}{-30} = -2$$
Step 5: Pratibimb ki unchai:
$$h_i = m \times h_o = -2 \times 5 = -10 \text{ cm}$$
Answer: Pratibimb darpan se 60 cm saamne, vaastavik, ulta aur 10 cm uncha hai.
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. 1.5 apvartnank wale kaach se bane ubhayottol lens ki dono vakrata trijya 20 cm hai. Lens ki focus doori gyat karo.
Given: n = 1.5, R₁ = +20 cm, R₂ = -20 cm. Ubhayottol ke liye R₁ positive, R₂ negative
Step 1: Lens maker formula:
$$\frac{1}{f} = (n-1)\left(\frac{1}{R_1} - \frac{1}{R_2}\right)$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$\frac{1}{f} = (1.5-1)\left(\frac{1}{20} - \frac{1}{-20}\right)$$
Step 3: Solve karo:
$$\frac{1}{f} = 0.5\left(\frac{1}{20} + \frac{1}{20}\right) = 0.5 \times \frac{2}{20}$$
$$\frac{1}{f} = 0.5 \times \frac{1}{10} = \frac{1}{20}$$
$$f = +20 \text{ cm}$$
Answer: Focus doori = +20 cm. Dhanyatmak chinh batata hai ki ye abhisari lens hai.
PYQ 2024, 2022, 2019
Q3. Prakash kiran hawa se paani me 45° ke aapatan kon par pravesh karti hai. Apvartan kon gyat karo. n_paani = 4/3, n_hawa = 1
Given: i = 45°, n₁ = 1, n₂ = 4/3
Step 1: Snell ka niyam:
$$n_1 \sin i = n_2 \sin r$$
Step 2: sin r ke liye solve karo:
$$\sin r = \frac{n_1 \sin i}{n_2} = \frac{1 \times \sin 45°}{4/3}$$
Step 3: sin45° = 1/√2 = 0.707:
$$\sin r = \frac{0.707}{1.333} = 0.530$$
Step 4: r nikalo:
$$r = \sin^{-1}(0.530) = 32°$$
Answer: Apvartan kon = 32°. Kiran abhilamb ki taraf jhukti hai kyunki paani sghan madhyam hai.
PYQ 2023, 2021
Q4. Ek sukshmadarshi ki abhidrishak lens ki focus doori 2 cm aur netrika ki 5 cm hai. Tube length 20 cm hai. Sukshmadarshi ki aavardhan kshamta gyat karo. D = 25 cm
Given: fₒ = 2 cm, fₑ = 5 cm, L = 20 cm, D = 25 cm
Step 1: Yaugik sukshmadarshi ki aavardhan kshamta:
$$M = M_o \times M_e = \frac{L}{f_o} \times \frac{D}{f_e}$$
Step 2: Abhidrishak ka aavardhan:
$$M_o = \frac{L}{f_o} = \frac{20}{2} = 10$$
Step 3: Netrika ka koniya aavardhan:
$$M_e = \frac{D}{f_e} = \frac{25}{5} = 5$$
Step 4: Kul aavardhan:
$$M = 10 \times 5 = 50$$
Answer: Sukshmadarshi ki aavardhan kshamta = 50. Yani vastu 50 guna badi dikhegi.
PYQ 2024, 2022
Q5. Ek durdarshi ki abhidrishak ki focus doori 100 cm aur netrika ki 2 cm hai. Durbadarshi ki aavardhan kshamta aur tube length gyat karo jab antim pratibimb anant par bane.
Given: fₒ = 100 cm, fₑ = 2 cm, Antim pratibimb anant par
Step 1: Anant par antim pratibimb ke liye durdarshi ki aavardhan kshamta:
$$M = -\frac{f_o}{f_e}$$
Step 2: Values rakho. Rinatmak chinh ulte pratibimb ko batata hai:
$$M = -\frac{100}{2} = -50$$
Step 3: Tube length anant par focusing ke liye:
$$L = f_o + f_e = 100 + 2 = 102 \text{ cm}$$
Answer: Aavardhan kshamta = 50, Tube length = 102 cm. Pratibimb ulta banega.
⚡ Board Tip: Sign convention yaad rakho: u hamesha negative, concave mirror/fₒ positive lens ke liye f positive. v negative = vaastavik, v positive = aabhaasi. Lens maker me R₁ light aane wali surface.
