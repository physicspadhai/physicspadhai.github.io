PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. Young ke dvijhiri prayog me jhiriyon ke beech ki doori 0.28 mm hai aur parda 1.4 m door hai. Kendriya deele se chauthi deele ki doori 1.2 cm hai. Upyog kiye gaye prakash ki tarang-dhairdhya gyat karo.
Given: d = 0.28 mm = 0.28 × 10⁻³ m, D = 1.4 m, n = 4, y₄ = 1.2 cm = 1.2 × 10⁻² m
Step 1: n vi deele ki doori ka sutra:
$$y_n = \frac{n\lambda D}{d}$$
Step 2: λ ke liye solve karo:
$$\lambda = \frac{y_n d}{n D}$$
Step 3: Values rakho:
$$\lambda = \frac{1.2 \times 10^{-2} \times 0.28 \times 10^{-3}}{4 \times 1.4}$$
Step 4: Calculate karo:
$$\lambda = \frac{0.336 \times 10^{-5}}{5.6} = 0.06 \times 10^{-5}$$
$$\lambda = 6.0 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m} = 600 \text{ nm}$$
Answer: Tarang-dhairdhya = 600 nm. Ye naurangi prakash hai.
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. Young ke prayog me 500 nm tarang-dhairdhya ke prakash ke liye fringe chaudaai 1 mm hai. Yadi poora upkaran 1.33 apvartnank wale paani me dooba diya jaye to nayi fringe chaudaai kya hogi?
Given: λ = 500 nm, β_hawa = 1 mm = 10⁻³ m, n = 1.33
Step 1: Hawa me fringe chaudaai:
$$\beta = \frac{\lambda D}{d}$$
Step 2: Paani me tarang-dhairdhya badal jayegi:
$$\lambda_{paani} = \frac{\lambda_{hawa}}{n}$$
Step 3: Paani me fringe chaudaai:
$$\beta_{paani} = \frac{\lambda_{paani} D}{d} = \frac{\lambda_{hawa} D}{n d} = \frac{\beta_{hawa}}{n}$$
Step 4: Values rakho:
$$\beta_{paani} = \frac{1 \text{ mm}}{1.33} = 0.75 \text{ mm}$$
Answer: Nayi fringe chaudaai = 0.75 mm. Fringe patli ho jayegi.
PYQ 2024, 2023, 2019
Q3. Ek single slit ka chaudaai 0.1 mm hai aur is par 600 nm ka prakash dala gaya hai. 2 m door parde par kendriya ucchishth ki chaudaai gyat karo.
Given: a = 0.1 mm = 0.1 × 10⁻³ m = 10⁻⁴ m, λ = 600 nm = 600 × 10⁻⁹ m, D = 2 m
Step 1: Single slit vivartan me pratham nimnishth ki sthiti:
$$a \sin \theta = \lambda$$
$$\sin \theta \approx \theta = \frac{\lambda}{a}$$
Step 2: Kendriya ucchishth ki chaudaai = 2 × pratham nimnishth ki doori:
$$\text{Chaudaai} = 2y_1 = 2 \times \frac{\lambda D}{a}$$
Step 3: Values rakho:
$$\text{Chaudaai} = 2 \times \frac{600 \times 10^{-9} \times 2}{10^{-4}}$$
Step 4: Calculate karo:
$$\text{Chaudaai} = 2 \times \frac{1200 \times 10^{-9}}{10^{-4}} = 2 \times 12 \times 10^{-3}$$
$$\text{Chaudaai} = 24 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m} = 24 \text{ mm}$$
Answer: Kendriya ucchishth ki chaudaai = 24 mm = 2.4 cm
PYQ 2023, 2022
Q4. Prakash hawa se kaach me Brewster kon par aapatit hota hai. Yadi paravartit kiran 90° par apvartit kiran se bane to kaach ka apvartnank gyat karo. i_B = 57°
Given: Brewster kon i_B = 57°, i_B + r = 90°
Step 1: Brewster ka niyam:
$$\tan i_B = n$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$n = \tan 57°$$
Step 3: tan 57° ka maan:
$$n = 1.54$$
Answer: Kaach ka apvartnank = 1.54. Is kon par paravartit prakash poornatah samtal dhrubit hota hai.
PYQ 2024, 2021
Q5. Do kalabaddh srot jinki teevrataon ka anupaat 9:1 hai, vyatikaran utpann karte hain. Uchchishth aur nimnishth par teevrataon ka anupaat gyat karo.
Given: I₁:I₂ = 9:1. Maana I₁ = 9I, I₂ = I
Step 1: Teevrata aayam ke varg ke samanupati:
$$\frac{I_1}{I_2} = \frac{a_1^2}{a_2^2} = \frac{9}{1}$$
$$\frac{a_1}{a_2} = \frac{3}{1}$$
Step 2: Uchchishth par parinami aayam:
$$a_{max} = a_1 + a_2 = 3a + a = 4a$$
Step 3: Nimnishth par parinami aayam:
$$a_{min} = a_1 - a_2 = 3a - a = 2a$$
Step 4: Teevrataon ka anupaat:
$$\frac{I_{max}}{I_{min}} = \frac{a_{max}^2}{a_{min}^2} = \frac{(4a)^2}{(2a)^2} = \frac{16}{4} = \frac{4}{1}$$
Answer: Uchchishth aur nimnishth par teevrataon ka anupaat = 4:1
⚡ Board Tip: YDSE me β = λD/d yaad rakho. Vivartan me kendriya max ki chaudaai 2λD/a hoti hai. Kalabaddh srot ke liye I ∝ a². Brewster angle par tan i_B = n.
