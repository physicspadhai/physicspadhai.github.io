PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. Do point charges +9e aur +e hawa me 16 cm ki doori par hain. Inke beech teesra charge q kahan rakhe ki wo equilibrium me rahe?
Given: q₁ = +9e, q₂ = +e, Distance = 16 cm = 0.16 m
Step 1: Maan lete hain q charge ko +9e se x doori par rakhte hain. To +e se doori = (16 - x) cm hogi.

Step 2: Equilibrium ke liye q par dono charges ke kaaran lagne wale bal barabar aur vipreet hone chahiye.
$$F_1 = F_2$$
Step 3: Coulomb's law lagao:
$$\frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\frac{(9e)(q)}{x^2} = \frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\frac{(e)(q)}{(16-x)^2}$$
Step 4: Common terms cancel karo:
$$\frac{9}{x^2} = \frac{1}{(16-x)^2}$$
Step 5: Cross multiply karo:
$$9(16-x)^2 = x^2$$
Step 6: Square root lo dono taraf:
$$3(16-x) = x$$
Step 7: Solve karo:
$$48 - 3x = x \Rightarrow 48 = 4x \Rightarrow x = 12 \text{ cm}$$
Answer: Charge q ko +9e se 12 cm aur +e se 4 cm door rakhna hoga.
PYQ 2023, 2021
Q2. 0.2 μC ke point charge se 10 cm door hawa me electric field ki teevrata gyat karo.
Given: q = 0.2 μC = 0.2 × 10⁻⁶ C, r = 10 cm = 0.1 m, $\frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0} = 9 \times 10^9$ N m²/C²
Step 1: Electric field ka formula:
$$E = \frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\frac{q}{r^2}$$
Step 2: Values substitute karo:
$$E = 9 \times 10^9 \times \frac{0.2 \times 10^{-6}}{(0.1)^2}$$
Step 3: Denominator solve karo: (0.1)² = 0.01
$$E = 9 \times 10^9 \times \frac{0.2 \times 10^{-6}}{0.01}$$
Step 4: Division karo: $\frac{0.2}{0.01} = 20$
$$E = 9 \times 10^9 \times 20 \times 10^{-6}$$
Step 5: Multiply karo:
$$E = 180 \times 10^3 = 1.8 \times 10^5 \text{ N/C}$$
Answer: Electric field = $1.8 \times 10^5$ N/C, charge se door ki taraf.
PYQ 2024, 2022
Q3. Ek electric dipole jiska dipole moment $4 \times 10^{-9}$ C-m hai, ke aksh par 0.2 m door electric field gyat karo.
Given: p = $4 \times 10^{-9}$ C-m, r = 0.2 m, r >> a maan lete hain
Step 1: Axial line par electric field ka formula:
$$E_{axial} = \frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\frac{2p}{r^3}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$E_{axial} = 9 \times 10^9 \times \frac{2 \times 4 \times 10^{-9}}{(0.2)^3}$$
Step 3: Cube nikalo: (0.2)³ = 0.008
$$E_{axial} = 9 \times 10^9 \times \frac{8 \times 10^{-9}}{0.008}$$
Step 4: Simplify karo: $\frac{8}{0.008} = 1000$
$$E_{axial} = 9 \times 10^9 \times 1000 \times 10^{-9}$$
Step 5: Powers solve karo: 10⁹ × 10⁻⁹ = 1
$$E_{axial} = 9 \times 1000 = 9000 \text{ N/C}$$
Answer: Electric field = 9000 N/C, dipole ki disha me.
PYQ 2024, 2023
Q4. $2 \times 10^{-8}$ C-m dipole moment ka dipole $2 \times 10^5$ N/C uniform field me 30° par rakha hai. Torque gyat karo.
Given: p = $2 \times 10^{-8}$ C-m, E = $2 \times 10^5$ N/C, θ = 30°
Step 1: Torque ka formula:
$$\tau = pE\sin\theta$$
Step 2: Values rakho: sin30° = 0.5
$$\tau = 2 \times 10^{-8} \times 2 \times 10^5 \times 0.5$$
Step 3: Multiply karo:
$$\tau = 4 \times 10^{-3} \times 0.5$$
$$\tau = 2 \times 10^{-3} \text{ N-m}$$
Answer: Torque = $2 \times 10^{-3}$ N-m. Ye dipole ko field ki disha me align karne ki koshish karega.
PYQ 2023, 2021
Q5. 10 cm bhuj wale cube ke kendra par 5μC charge rakha hai. Cube ke ek falak se guzarne wala electric flux kitna hoga?
Given: q = 5μC = 5 × 10⁻⁶ C, $\varepsilon_0 = 8.854 \times 10^{-12}$ C²/N m²
Step 1: Gauss law se total flux poore cube ke liye:
$$\phi_{total} = \frac{q_{enclosed}}{\varepsilon_0}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$\phi_{total} = \frac{5 \times 10^{-6}}{8.854 \times 10^{-12}}$$
Step 3: Calculate karo:
$$\phi_{total} = 5.646 \times 10^5 \text{ N m²/C}$$
Step 4: Cube ke 6 falak hain aur charge kendra par hai, isliye flux sabhi falakon me barabar batega:
$$\phi_{ek falak} = \frac{\phi_{total}}{6} = \frac{5.646 \times 10^5}{6}$$
$$\phi_{ek falak} = 9.41 \times 10^4 \text{ N m²/C}$$
Answer: Ek falak se guzarne wala flux = $9.4 \times 10^4$ N m²/C
