PYQ 2024, 2023, 2022
Q1. 2 μC ke charge ko 0.2 m door le jane me kitna karya karna padega, yadi ye karya $8 \times 10^5$ N/C ke uniform electric field ke viruddh kiya jaye?
Given: q = 2 μC = 2 × 10⁻⁶ C, E = 8 × 10⁵ N/C, d = 0.2 m, θ = 180° kyunki field ke viruddh
Step 1: Electric field me charge par lagne wala bal:
$$F = qE = 2 \times 10^{-6} \times 8 \times 10^5 = 1.6 \text{ N}$$
Step 2: Karya ka formula jab bal aur visthapan ke beech kon θ ho:
$$W = Fd\cos\theta$$
Step 3: Field ke viruddh le jane par θ = 180°, aur cos180° = -1. Bahari agent dwara karya dhanyatmak hoga:
$$W_{ext} = qEd = 2 \times 10^{-6} \times 8 \times 10^5 \times 0.2$$
Step 4: Calculate karo:
$$W_{ext} = 16 \times 10^{-1} \times 0.2 = 3.2 \times 10^{-1} = 0.32 \text{ J}$$
Answer: Karya = 0.32 J karna padega.
PYQ 2023, 2021, 2020
Q2. 12 pF ke capacitor ko 50 V ki battery se joda gaya hai. Capacitor me sansthit aavesh aur urja gyat karo.
Given: C = 12 pF = 12 × 10⁻¹² F, V = 50 V
Step 1: Capacitor par aavesh ka formula:
$$Q = CV$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$Q = 12 \times 10^{-12} \times 50 = 600 \times 10^{-12} = 6 \times 10^{-10} \text{ C}$$
Step 3: Sansthit urja ka formula:
$$U = \frac{1}{2}CV^2$$
Step 4: Values rakho:
$$U = \frac{1}{2} \times 12 \times 10^{-12} \times (50)^2$$
Step 5: Solve karo: (50)² = 2500
$$U = 6 \times 10^{-12} \times 2500 = 15000 \times 10^{-12}$$
$$U = 1.5 \times 10^{-8} \text{ J}$$
Answer: Aavesh = $6 \times 10^{-10}$ C, Urja = $1.5 \times 10^{-8}$ J
PYQ 2024, 2022, 2019
Q3. 2μF, 3μF aur 6μF ke teen capacitor shreni kram me jode gaye hain. Sanyojan ki tulya dharita gyat karo. Yadi 100V lagaye jaye to 2μF par vibhavantar kitna hoga?
Given: C₁ = 2μF, C₂ = 3μF, C₃ = 6μF, V = 100V, Shreni kram
Step 1: Shreni kram me tulya dharita:
$$\frac{1}{C_s} = \frac{1}{C_1} + \frac{1}{C_2} + \frac{1}{C_3}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$\frac{1}{C_s} = \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{3+2+1}{6} = \frac{6}{6} = 1$$
Step 3: Isliye Cs = 1 μF

Step 4: Shreni kram me har capacitor par aavesh saman hota hai:
$$Q = C_s V = 1 \times 10^{-6} \times 100 = 10^{-4} \text{ C}$$
Step 5: 2μF par vibhavantar:
$$V_1 = \frac{Q}{C_1} = \frac{10^{-4}}{2 \times 10^{-6}} = \frac{100}{2} = 50 \text{ V}$$
Answer: Tulya dharita = 1 μF, 2μF par vibhavantar = 50 V
PYQ 2023, 2021
Q4. Samantar plate capacitor ki plate ka kshetrafal 100 cm² hai aur unke beech ki doori 2 mm hai. Yadi iske beech K = 5 paravaidhyutank ka padarth bhar diya jaye to nayi dharita kya hogi? ε₀ = 8.85×10⁻¹² C²/N m²
Given: A = 100 cm² = 100 × 10⁻⁴ m² = 10⁻² m², d = 2 mm = 2 × 10⁻³ m, K = 5
Step 1: Hawa wale capacitor ki dharita:
$$C_0 = \frac{\varepsilon_0 A}{d}$$
Step 2: Values rakho:
$$C_0 = \frac{8.85 \times 10^{-12} \times 10^{-2}}{2 \times 10^{-3}}$$
Step 3: Solve karo:
$$C_0 = \frac{8.85 \times 10^{-14}}{2 \times 10^{-3}} = 4.425 \times 10^{-11} \text{ F} = 44.25 \text{ pF}$$
Step 4: Paravaidhyut ke sath nayi dharita:
$$C = K C_0 = 5 \times 44.25 = 221.25 \text{ pF}$$
Answer: Nayi dharita = 221.25 pF
PYQ 2024, 2022
Q5. Do samrup golon A aur B par kramshah +10μC aur -6μC aavesh hai. Inhe sparsh karakar alag kar diya jata hai. Ab har gole par aavesh kitna hoga?
Given: Q₁ = +10μC, Q₂ = -6μC, Gole samrup hain
Step 1: Sparsh karane par kul aavesh dono golon par saman roop se bat jata hai kyunki gole samrup hain.

Step 2: Kul aavesh gyat karo:
$$Q_{total} = Q_1 + Q_2 = 10 + (-6) = 4 \mu\text{C}$$
Step 3: Sparsh ke baad har gole par aavesh:
$$Q = \frac{Q_{total}}{2} = \frac{4}{2} = 2 \mu\text{C}$$
Answer: Sparsh ke baad har gole par +2μC aavesh hoga.
⚡ Board Tip: Capacitor ke numerical me hamesha unit conversion karo: μF = 10⁻⁶ F, pF = 10⁻¹² F. Shreni me Q same, samanantar me V same.
